Interested in joining Troop 463? These are the steps that each scout family needs to complete.

 

1.    Join our Troop on a Rim Rover Hike. Details will be emailed in January. If you know your son wants to join Troop 463 or is thinking about joining the troop, he should be on this hike. The next qualifying hike could be a month away. We are an outdoor troop and hiking is a big part of having fun on the outings. A parent or guardian must accompany each scout.

 

2.    Prepare your scout for the Scoutmaster Conference

These items must be completed prior to scheduling the Scoutmaster conference.

-      Complete "How to Protect Your Children from Child Abuse: A Parent's Guide" https://www.scouting.org/filestore/ypt/pdf/100-015.pdf

-      Complete and turn in the Health History (medical forms part A&B) https://www.scouting.org/filestore/HealthSafety/pdf/parts_ab.pdf

-      Complete and turn in Youth Registration Form

 

3.    Schedule your son's Scoutmaster Conference during WeST.

a.     Mr. Davis will be available from 8:30am – 2:00pm to meet with those boys who are ready to pass their Scout requirements, as specified in the link below: https://usscouts.org/advance/boyscout/bsrank1.asp

b.     The scout should be prepared to:

                                               i.     Demonstrate how to tie a square knot and what it is used for.

                                              ii.     Describe the scout badge.

 

4.    Parent Commitment to the Troop.

Parents play a critical role in the Troop and in the success of their son's scouting experience! At a minimum, we ask all parents to commit to the following:

4.1 Full participation in all fundraising events.

4.1.1    One shift per scout at Hands on the Arts in May

4.1.2    Two shifts per scout during Sunnyvale Art and Wine held the first weekend of June

4.1.3    Support at any events added on by the Parent Committee.

                      ( A shift includes 1 scout and at least 1 parent)

4.2 Attend one Board of Review each quarter

4.3 Attend Parents Committee Meeting (2nd Thursday of each month) 1 per quarter expected as a minimum.

 

5.     Additional troop paperwork will be collected after the Scoutmaster has signed off on the Scout Requirement.

 

We look forward to having your family as part of Troop 463.
