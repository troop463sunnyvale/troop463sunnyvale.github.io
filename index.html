Troop 463: Boy Scout Troop in Sunnyvale, California

Join Us

Have Fun

We value fun as one key part of Boy Scouts and our Troop. We want scouts to enjoy participating in all aspects of the program and being part of a cohesive group.

Learn Outdoor Skills

We emphasize the learning of outdoor skills, such as cooking, camping, knot-tying, hiking, first aid, swimming, fire-building, conservation, and service.

Personal Growth

We believe that the true purpose of participating in Scouting is to grow as a person, to learn leadership skills, and to develop self-confidence and perseverance.

We meet every Tuesday at 7:30 PM. Be there!

Location: Sunnyvale United Methodist Church | Email: troop463info@gmail.com
