2024 November: Tomales Bay Kayak Camping

Carry all our gear on kayaks to get to our campsite!

Day 1:

  • Head to Heart’s Desire Beach
  • Rent kayaks from Blue Water Kayaking
  • Paddle to the Marshall Beach Group Boat-Only campsite
  • Lunch, Setup Tent, Activity etc, spend the night at campsite

Day 2:

  • Paddle back to Heart’s Desire Beach
  • Return kayaks
  • Lunch?
  • Head home
