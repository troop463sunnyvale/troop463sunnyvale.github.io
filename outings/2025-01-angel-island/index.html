2025 January: Angel Island Backpacking

Urban and outdoors backpacking combined: ride the train and ferry to camp at Angel Island.

Day 1

  • Meet at Caltrain station 7am (NOT CHURCH)
  • Take the 7:42am train
  • Arrive at San Francisco Caltrain Station (Address: 700 4th St, San Francisco, CA 94107)
  • Head to Ferry Building (Address: 1 Ferry Building, San Francisco, CA 94105)
  • Walk to Ferry Building (~30 min/1.6 miles)
  • Buy round trip tickets to and from Angel Island
  • Take the 10:15 AM Ferry to Angel Island
  • Lunch (on island)
  • Visit museum before close at 2:30
  • Hike to Garrison Group Campsite( ~1 mile/ ~30min)
  • Setup tent
  • Dinner
  • Campfire Activities/Night Games - Led by Kaden and potentially others

Day 2

  • Wake up 7am
  • Early breakfast/cleanup
  • Take the 10:55 AM Ferry to SF Ferry Building
  • Walk back to Caltrain
