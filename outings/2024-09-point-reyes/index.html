2024 September: Point Reyes Backpacking

Our first, "easy" backpacking outing for the season, at scenic Point Reyes.

Day 1:

  • Arrive at trailhead, Unload all gear, drivers with cars go down to Limantour Beach Parking, (eat dinner)
  • Drivers come back in one car, park at trailhead
  • Start hiking to sky camp
  • Setup camp and go to sleep

Day 2:

  • Wakeup, breakfast
  • Pack up camp, start hiking to Coast camp
  • Set bags down, walk down to Secret beach
  • Arrive back at camp for dinner
  • Night game and campfire
  • Quiet time

Day 3:

  • Wakeup, breakfast
  • Pack up and start hiking on the beach back to cars
  • Arrive at cars, send two drivers to retrieve other car at trailhead
  • Pack up and go home
  • Maybe lunch
