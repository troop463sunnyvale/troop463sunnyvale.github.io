40th Anniversary Memorabilia

As many of you know, we gathered stories and photos and created a Memory Book of the last 40 years. Stories from the first 25 years are woven together with the years that followed. I bet some of you don't even remember the stories you told back in 1993. It was fun to read and some of you, when asked again for stories, almost told the same stories... now that's leaving an impression.

This 55+ page Memory Book includes:

- Troop History
- Stories from all generations of the troop
- Photos from different time periods of some of your favorite outings
- News articles about the troop
- Wadatchoo Cheer
- An updated list of all of our Eagle scouts

The DVD includes:

- A video taken at the 40th Anniversary event with interviews of some of your dear friends
- A slide show of the 40th Anniversary event
- The 40th Anniversary Memory Book (as a pdf file)
- The slide show shown at the 40th Anniversary event
- The 25th Anniversary Memory Book (as a pdf file)
- Scanned versions of Troop Newsletters dating back as far as 1988
- A troop roster with peoples' names, city, state, email, and phone (unless requested not to)
